The Bazario platform is envisioned as a modern, bilingual (English/French) auction marketplace. Key requirements include:
**Platform Foundation:** Responsive design (mobile-first), dark/light mode with persistence, modern UI with specific gradient buttons and animations, PWA capabilities, SEO, accessibility.
**User Management:** Personal (single listing, 5% fee) and Business accounts (multiple items, 4.5% fee), both requiring extensive personal/company details and 2FA. Includes profile management with avatar upload and account type switching.
**Marketplace Core:** Advanced search (location-based, category, price, date, condition, radius, map integration), real-time auction (countdown, dynamic bids, Buy It Now, confetti for wins, bid history), and user-friendly image upload.
**Dashboards:** Seller (commission notice, listing management, buyer communication, Promote Listing feature, analytics) and Buyer (wishlist, won items, purchase history, seller communication, reporting).
**Advanced Features:** Affiliate program (referral links, earnings, withdrawals), promotional system (paid listing promotion with targeting, badges, approval), comprehensive communication (full-page messaging, two-column layout, notifications, file attachments, read receipts), and a floating AI Support Assistant.
**Security & Compliance:** Secure payment processing (Stripe, mandatory credit card verification, AVS checks,  authorization hold), GDPR compliance, fraud prevention, user verification, and one-click reporting.
**Monetization & Onboarding:** Seller promotion tiers, commission structure, optimized seller registration, and buyer activation.
**UI/UX Enhancements:** Bento Grid layouts, kinetic typography, Top Seller/Hot Item features on the homepage, Google Ads integration, and a detailed footer.
