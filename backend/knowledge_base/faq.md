## Bidding Questions

**Q: How does bidding work?**
A:
1. Find item you want
2. Check current high bid
3. Enter your maximum bid amount
4. System auto-bids for you up to your max
5. Get notified if outbid
6. Win when timer reaches zero

**Q: What is proxy bidding?**
A: You set your maximum bid, and our system automatically increases your bid by the minimum increment only when needed to stay competitive. You never pay more than necessary up to your max.

**Q: Can I retract a bid?**
A: No, all bids are binding commitments. Please bid carefully and only on items you intend to purchase.

**Q: Why was my bid rejected?**
A: Common reasons:
- Bid too low (must beat current + minimum increment)
- Account not verified (phone + payment required)
- You're the seller (cannot bid on own listings)
- Buy Now disabled by admin
- Auction has ended

**Q: What does "Your bid must be at least $X.XX to lead" mean?**
A: Your bid needs to be higher than the current high bid plus the minimum bid increment to become the new leader.

**Q: Why did I get outbid immediately after bidding?**
A: Another bidder set a higher maximum bid using proxy bidding. The system automatically outbid you up to their max.

## Anti-Sniping & Timing

**Q: Why did the auction timer suddenly extend?**
A: That's our anti-sniping protection! When someone bids in the final 2 minutes, the auction extends by 2 minutes from the bid time. This ensures fair opportunity for all bidders.

**Q: How many times can the auction extend?**
A: Unlimited. As long as bids keep coming in the final 2 minutes, the auction keeps extending by 2 minutes each time.

**Q: Does extension affect all lots in multi-item auctions?**
A: No! Each lot has its own independent timer. Extension on Lot 1 does not affect Lot 2's end time.

**Q: Is there a way to prevent extensions?**
A: No, anti-sniping is a core fairness feature that cannot be disabled. It protects against last-second bidding manipulation.

**Q: What if I can't stay for extensions?**
A: Set your maximum proxy bid at your true maximum. The system will bid for you automatically during any extensions.

## Shipping & Pickup

**Q: Do you ship items?**
A: Shipping availability depends on the individual seller. **Local pickup is the default** for all items.

**Q: How do I know if shipping is available?**
A: Look for the **Shipping Icon** (üì¶ or truck symbol) on the lot detail page:
- **Icon Present** = Seller offers shipping
- **No Icon** = Local pickup only (buyer must collect)

**Q: Can I request shipping if there's no icon?**
A: You can contact the seller via messaging system, but they are not obligated to offer shipping. Never assume shipping is available without confirmation.

**Q: Who pays for shipping?**
A: Shipping costs are determined by the seller and typically paid by the buyer. Details are in the listing description.

**Q: Does BidVex handle shipping?**
A: No, BidVex does not ship items. Shipping arrangements are between buyer and seller. We provide the messaging platform for coordination.

**Q: What if the item arrives damaged?**
A: Contact the seller first. If unresolved, create a support ticket. Buyer protection may apply depending on circumstances.

## Buy Now Feature

**Q: What is Buy Now?**
A: Buy Now lets you purchase an item instantly at a fixed price without bidding. Look for the ‚ö° "Buy Now Available" badge.

**Q: Why use Buy Now instead of bidding?**
A: 
- Skip competitive bidding
- Guaranteed purchase
- Instant transaction
- No waiting for auction to end
- No risk of being outbid

**Q: Can all items be bought now?**
A: No, only lots where the seller enabled Buy Now and the admin hasn't disabled the feature globally.

**Q: What happens after Buy Now purchase?**
A:
1. Payment processed immediately
2. Lot marked as "Sold Out"
3. Automatic handshake conversation created
4. Coordinate pickup/shipping with seller
5. Item removed from bidding

**Q: Is Buy Now price negotiable?**
A: No, Buy Now is a fixed instant purchase price. If you want a lower price, participate in bidding instead.

**Q: Why is Buy Now disabled sometimes?**
A: Admin can disable Buy Now globally via marketplace settings for special events or promotional periods.

## Multi-Item Lots

**Q: What are multi-item lots?**
A: A single auction containing multiple independent lots (items), each with its own pricing, timer, and bidding.

**Q: Can I bid on just one lot?**
A: Yes! Each lot is completely independent. Bid on one, some, or all lots as you prefer.

**Q: Do lot timers sync?**
A: No, each lot has its own independent timer and anti-sniping protection.

**Q: Can I Buy Now on one lot and bid on another in the same auction?**
A: Absolutely! Each lot's Buy Now is independent.

**Q: What does "Sold Out" mean on a lot?**
A: Someone purchased that lot via Buy Now. It's no longer available for bidding.

**Q: Who can create multi-item auctions?**
A: By default, only business accounts. However, admin can enable "Allow All Users Multi-Lot" to permit personal accounts.

**Q: What is buyer premium?**
A: A percentage fee added to the final hammer price:
- Personal accounts: 5%
- Business accounts: 4.5%

**Q: Example calculation?**
A: Item final bid: $100
- Personal buyer total: $105 ($100 + 5%)

**Q: What payment methods do you accept?**
A: All major credit and debit cards via Stripe secure processing. No cash, checks, or wire transfers.

**Q: When am I charged?**
A: Immediately after winning the auction (when timer reaches zero and you have high bid).

**Q: Can I get a refund?**
A: Refunds are case-by-case:
- Item not as described: Eligible
- Seller doesn't deliver: Full refund
- Changed mind: No refund (bids are binding)
- Damaged item: Buyer protection may apply

**Q: How do sellers get paid?**
A: Sellers receive payout after successful transaction, minus BidVex success fees. Processed through Stripe.
