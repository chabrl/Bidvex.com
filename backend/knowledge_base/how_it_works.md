## Getting Started: Your Path to Winning

### Step 1: Browse & Discover
Explore thousands of unique items across multiple categories:
- **Single Item Auctions:** Traditional one-item listings
- **Multi-Item Lots:** Multiple items in one auction
- **Buy Now Items:** Instant purchase options
- **Promoted Listings:** Featured premium items

**Browsing Tips:**
- Use search filters (category, price, location)
- Watch for promoted items (‚≠ê Premium, üëë Elite badges)
- Check "Ending Soon" for urgent opportunities
- Look for shipping icon (üì¶) if you need delivery

### Step 2: Register Free
Create your BidVex account in minutes:
1. Click "Sign Up" or "Create Account"
2. Enter email and create password
3. Complete profile information
4. **Verify Phone Number** (SMS code)
5. **Add Payment Method** (required for bidding)

**Account Types:**
- **Personal:** Individual buyers/sellers (5% buyer premium)
- **Business:** Commercial accounts (4.5% buyer premium)

### Step 3: Place Your Bid
Ready to compete? Here's how:

**Bidding Process:**
1. Navigate to item detail page
2. Review current high bid and time remaining
3. Enter your maximum bid amount
4. System auto-bids up to your max (proxy bidding)
5. Get real-time notifications on bid status

**Important Bidding Info:**
- Bids must exceed current price + minimum increment
- Anti-sniping protection may extend auction time
- You can be outbid at any moment
- Verify phone + payment before bidding

**Buy Now Alternative:**
- Look for ‚ö° "Buy Now Available" badge
- Skip bidding entirely
- Purchase instantly at fixed price
- Immediate handshake with seller

### Step 4: Win the Auction
When the timer hits zero:

**If You Won:**
- Email confirmation sent
- Payment processed automatically
- Handshake conversation created with seller
- Coordinate pickup or shipping
- Leave seller rating after transaction

**If You Lost:**
- Notification of final outcome
- Browse similar items suggestion
- No charges applied
- Try again on next auction

### Step 5: Secure Payment
BidVex uses Stripe for secure processing:

**Payment Flow:**
1. Winning bid + buyer premium calculated
2. Charge to saved payment method
3. Instant confirmation email
4. Invoice generated automatically
5. Seller receives payout (minus fees)

**Payment Security:**
- PCI DSS compliant
- End-to-end encryption
- Buyer protection program
- Dispute resolution available

## FAQ Quick Reference

**Q: Do you offer shipping on all items?**
A: No, local pickup is default. Check for shipping icon (üì¶) on lot detail page.

**Q: Why did the auction timer extend?**
A: Anti-sniping protection. Bids in final 2 minutes extend timer by 2 minutes.

**Q: How do I verify my account?**
A: Settings ‚Üí Verify Phone (SMS) ‚Üí Add Payment Method ‚Üí Get verified badge.

**Q: What happens if I'm outbid?**
A: You receive instant notification. You can bid again if auction still active.

**Q: Can I cancel my bid?**
A: No, bids are binding commitments. Bid carefully.

**Q: How do promoted listings work?**
A: Premium ($25, 7 days) or Elite ($50, 14 days) gets priority placement.

**Q: What's the buyer premium?**
A: 5% for personal, 4.5% for business accounts. Added to final price.

**Q: How do I contact the seller?**
A: Use built-in messaging system. Automatic handshake after winning.

**Q: What if there's a problem?**
A: Contact support@bidvex.com or create support ticket via AI assistant.
